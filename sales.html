// HARO — 5 шаблонов (GPT — короче и острее) · Старт: Июль 2026
Шаблон 1 — AI for Small Business
AI gives small businesses leverage. The biggest win is not replacing people — it is eliminating repetitive admin work, missed leads, and delayed responses. Even a simple automation stack can save 5–10 hours weekly and improve lead conversion through faster follow-up. The most impactful starting point is automating lead qualification and first response — two areas where speed directly impacts revenue.
Шаблон 2 — Sales Technology Trends
Sales tech is shifting from dashboards to action systems. Businesses no longer want data only — they want systems that automatically respond, route, qualify, and schedule. The 2026 trend is agentic AI: systems that take action based on triggers, not just provide insights.
Шаблон 3 — Lead Generation with AI
The best use of AI in lead generation is qualification speed. Companies waste budget buying leads they never contact properly. An AI qualification system that responds in under 2 minutes and scores leads automatically can increase meeting booking rates by 40–65% without increasing headcount.
Шаблон 4 — Chatbot ROI
A chatbot ROI should be measured by booked calls, response speed, support deflection, and captured contacts — not vanity engagement metrics. The systems delivering the highest ROI combine qualification logic, CRM sync, and booking automation in one flow rather than isolated tools.
Шаблон 5 — Workflow Automation
Automation works best when it removes bottlenecks, not when it adds complexity. The rule I follow: simplify first, automate second. The most common mistake is automating a broken process — you just get broken results faster. Map the ideal flow first, then build automation around it.
